Quantum Security Market Revenue Overview

The quantum security market was valued at just under 500 million U.S. dollars in 2022, with forecasts suggesting that this is likely to rise to 9.8 billion U.S. dollars by 2030. The majority of quantum security revenue is generated by the post-quantum security or cryptography (PQC) method.

Key trends driving this growth include:

  • Increased adoption of quantum cryptography methods for data security.
  • Significant investments in quantum technology by governments and enterprises.
  • Growing demand for post-quantum secure solutions to combat cyber threats.
